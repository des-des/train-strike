Trains in the UK are too expensive and it's no joke. The average ticket price has risen each year since 1997, often far exceeding the rate of inflation and rising three times faster than the average pay rise for the same period.

It's time to take action. It's time for the people to make their voices heard and demand an end to the rising prices. It's time to boycott the private train networks in the United Kingdom.

Until our train services are brought into public ownership, we, the undersigned, pledge not to use any privately-owned train services in this country.
